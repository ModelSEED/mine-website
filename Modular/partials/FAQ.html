Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't the compounds database include stereochemistry?

BNICE does not handle chirality currently. As such, where multiple stereoisomers of a structure exist, the MINE entry will include information on all of the isomers.

How can I integrate your database into my tool/pipeline?

We offer client APIs in JavaScript, Python and Perl which cover all the functionality of this website. Please contact us to request the most recent version. Alternatively, you may request all the unannotated(license restrictions, sorry) structures as a SDF file.

I found an error/ I'm skeptical about the validity of a compound

We would love to hear about it. Please let us know by sending an email to minedatabasescontact@gmail.com and let us know. If reproting a bug, please include information as to how the error was generated.
